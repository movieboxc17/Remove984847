Biography

Hi, I'm Elliot.

I am a 15-year-old photographer with a focus on capturing everything that catches my eye. For me, photography isn't just a hobby; it's a way to explore the world and preserve the beauty of everyday life.

I have a deep love for architecture, urban structures, and street photography. There is something fascinating about the way buildings interact with light and how the energy of a city can be frozen in a single frame.

Beyond the city streets, I love capturing the stillness of nature and animals. I am constantly exploring new places and I make sure to bring my camera wherever I go. Traveling is a massive part of my inspiration, allowing me to document new cultures and landscapes through my lens.
